About the Resort

The San Ignacio Resort Hotel is a beautiful and peaceful luxury resort located just minutes from the town of San Ignacio in the Cayo District. It has been serving guests since 1976 and offers amazing experiences, nature, and relaxation all in one place.

One of its most famous attractions is the Green Iguana Conservation Project, where visitors can learn about wildlife conservation and see iguanas up close. The resort also features a restaurant, pool, spa, and exciting tours around Belize.

Things You Can Do at San Ignacio Resort Hotel

  • Relax at the spa
